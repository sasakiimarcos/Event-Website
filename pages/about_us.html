About Us

Who we Are

We are a very diverse and experienced team and have been organizing Techcon for over 5 years. We have come a long way from our first event held, which what seems today, a long time ago. Since then, our team has grown considerably and the quality of our conferences and summits have improved greatly over the years. Today, our team does its very best to offer the most memorable experiences possible to attendees by bringing in the most influential and knowledgeable people in the tech industry. We have the intention to keep on improving every single year by attracting more and more companies and better speakers for our conference.

Mission and Vision

Our mission has always been to offer people a place where they could learn and find out about the latest trends in the tech industry by gathering the leading experts in the relevant fields of said industry. We believe that by gathering so many well-informed people in one place, it would facilitate the spread of knowledge across a lot of people.

Our vision is a more diverse and inclusive tech industry where people of all sorts of backgrounds can come together and work towards the improvement of the world.

About the Conference

Of all Techcon events done thus far, this year's "Techcon 2026" will be the biggest as we will be having over 15 different guest speakers and over 10 companies at our event, exceeding last year's 13 speakers and 8 companies. Not only that, but this year's event will also be hosted in the wonderful country of Somalia known for its widespread poverty, civil wars, territorial conflicts, and unstable government. The conference will be held at Mogadishu International Hotel in the nation's capital city.
